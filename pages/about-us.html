ABOUT US

Pistacho is a family business that we created in 2022. We are four members: two cousins with their mothers.
The four of us are very enterprising women since we were girls and we had the beautiful idea of getting together to create this venture together that we love so much.
We are passionate about what we do and we share our love for handbags. The four of us have gone shopping together countless times, and when it comes to handbags, our motto has always been the same: “They are NEVER enough!”.
Today, we uphold our motto and share it with you.
Thank you to all our loyal clients for trusting us and continuing to choose us.
Because they are never enough,
PISTACHO.

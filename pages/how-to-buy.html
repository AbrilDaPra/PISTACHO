HOW TO BUY

1. Choose the product you want to buy.
2. Click the "ADD TO CART" button. This will add the product to your cart and take you to it.
3. You can continue adding other products to the cart or else click "PAY".
4. Complete your contact information, select the shipping method you want, payment method and click "Continue".
5. Shipments are made through CORREO ARGENTINO (click to see prices).
6. Finally, on the Purchase Confirmation page you can review all the purchase information. Then click "Continue."
7. There you will be redirected to another screen so that you can fill in the information about the chosen payment method. After confirming the purchase you will receive an email from us.
8. Once the payment is confirmed, we will make the corresponding shipment of the products you have purchased.
9. If you have any questions before finalizing the purchase, you can write to us by WhatsApp on the green button on the right of the screen.
